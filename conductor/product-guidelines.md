# LuxeEstate Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
LuxeEstate's copy and communication should be **Concise & Informative**.
- **Clarity:** Provide essential information clearly and directly, avoiding unnecessary jargon.
- **Efficiency:** Respect the user's time by getting to the point quickly, especially in search and filtering interfaces.
- **Tone:** Professional and helpful, maintaining a sense of reliability and expertise in the luxury real estate market.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
The primary UX principle is a **Visual-First Experience**.
- **High-Quality Media:** Properties must be showcased through high-resolution photography and video, as visuals are the primary driver of engagement.
- **Minimal Clutter:** Keep the interface clean and focused, ensuring that property content always takes center stage.
- **Intuitive Navigation:** Users should be able to move seamlessly between property discovery, search, and details without friction.

## Design & Aesthetics
LuxeEstate follows a **Strict Color Palette** to ensure a premium and cohesive brand identity.
- **Primary Accent:** `#06f9d0` (Primary) for key calls to action and highlights.
- **Secondary Colors:** `#EEF6F6` (Background Light) and `#19322F` (Nordic Dark) for primary backgrounds and text, creating a sophisticated contrast.
- **Iconography:** Use Material Symbols Outlined for a modern and consistent visual language across all features.

## Accessibility & Responsiveness
We are committed to a **Mobile-First & Responsive** design philosophy.
- **Device Support:** The application must be fully functional and visually stunning on all screen sizes, from mobile phones to large desktop monitors.
- **Performance:** Ensure fast loading times and smooth interactions, particularly for image-heavy property pages.
- **Readability:** Maintain high contrast and clear typography to ensure the interface is accessible to all users.
