# LuxeEstate Product Guide

## Initial Concept
LuxeEstate is a premium real estate application designed to provide a high-end property discovery experience.

## Target Audience
The primary focus is on **High-end Buyers**—individuals seeking premium properties with an emphasis on luxury, quality, and a superior user experience.

## Product Goals
- **Property Discovery:** To create a visually stunning and intuitive platform where users can easily explore and discover their next high-end property.
- **Visual Presentation:** To present property listings with high-fidelity visuals and clear, detailed information.

## Core Features
- **Property Details View:** A rich, immersive view of each property, showcasing images, amenities, and detailed descriptions to help buyers make informed decisions.
- **Home Discover Screen:** A central hub for property search and discovery (as outlined in the initial project tasks).

## Visual Identity & UX
The platform will follow a **Nordic Minimalist** design aesthetic:
- **Style:** Clean, spacious, and sophisticated, using a refined color palette (Primary: #06f9d0, Background Light: #EEF6F6, Nordic Dark: #19322F).
- **Interactions:** Smooth, modern feedback that reinforces the premium feel of the brand.
